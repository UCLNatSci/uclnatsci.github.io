Natural Sciences PGTA Opportunities 26/27

We are currently accepting applications for PGTA positions during Terms 1 and 2 of the 26/27 academic year. Please read the job description(s) carefully before applying using the online application form. Positions will be filled on a rolling basis, so early applications are encouraged.

Decisions on shortlisting will be made on your detailed answers to questions 4 and 5. We are looking for candidates with a keen interest in applying and developing their teaching skills, so consider how your skills, experience and interests are relevant to the role before answering.

Shortlisted candidates will be invited to interview before being offered a position.

For informal enquiries, email the relevant module lead or natsci@ucl.ac.uk.
